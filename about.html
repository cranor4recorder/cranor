Why Me?

I have a proven record of public service in Monroe County

History of service

  • I have served the Monroe County Recorder, as Chief Deputy, for 7 years
  • I have worked to preserve our historic governmental archives for 10 years
  • I have served on the Monroe County Health Board for 12 years
  • I led the county’s energy efforts. This effort led to the installation of, at the time, the largest solar array on any county building in the Midwest.
  • I led the 2008 flood recovery efforts. This effort captured over $2 million in grant funds and helped over 100 families and individuals recover from the flood.
